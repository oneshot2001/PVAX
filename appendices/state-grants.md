# Appendix A — State School Safety Grant Programs

> Companion to PVAX `outcomes.grant_outcome.program` field
> Verified: 2026-05-03 via OpenAI Deep Research, primary federal/state sources only
> Coverage: 15 states (TX, FL, CA, NY, PA, OH, MI, GA, NC, AZ, CO, TN, VA, IL, NJ)
> **Confidence flags:** entries marked `MEDIUM` require independent re-verification against enacted budget bills or agency award ledgers before being used as definitive references in proposals or grant applications. Re-verification cadence: every 90 days, or when a state agency publishes a new fiscal-year NOFO.

---

## How to use this appendix

PVAX records grant outcomes in `outcomes.grant_outcome` per facility. This appendix tells you which state programs to consider when a facility is in a given state. Use the citation URLs at the time of application — programs change every fiscal year and continuing resolutions affect mid-year amounts.

**The federal layer (covered separately in README.md):**

- **FEMA NSGP FY2025** — $274.5M total ($137.25M NSGP-UA + $137.25M NSGP-S). ALN 97.008. Opportunity DHS-25-GPD-008-00-99. State Administrative Agencies apply; nonprofits are subapplicants.
- **DOJ COPS SVPP FY2025** — up to $73M solicitation; FY2025 awards 211 totaling $74,785,459. ALN 16.071. Opportunity O-COPS-2025-172379. Max $500k federal share, 25% local cash match. Does not fund SRO positions.

## What this appendix doesn't cover (yet)

- **Tribal nation school safety programs** — Bureau of Indian Education and tribal-administered grants are out of scope for v0.3; planned for v0.4.
- **All 50 states** — the 15 selected are the largest by student population plus high-growth/high-incident states. Coverage will expand as PVAX adoption demands it.
- **Private K-12 grant pools** — foundation and family-office grants (Sandy Hook Promise, etc.) are tracked in `distribution-and-pricing.md`, not here.
- **Higher-education grants** — DHS Campus Resilience Program, Title IV-B, and state higher-ed-specific safety funding are planned for a separate appendix.
- **Healthcare, cannabis, marina, equine, and other underserved verticals** — separate vertical appendices on the v0.3+ roadmap.

## Maintenance

This appendix is auto-stale. Each row should be re-verified at one of these triggers:

1. State agency publishes a new fiscal-year NOFO
2. State legislature enacts a new biennial budget
3. Federal continuing resolution affects state pass-through funding
4. 90 days have elapsed since last verification

When re-verifying: pull from the cited primary source URL; do not pull from grant-writing service pages, vendor blogs, or AI summaries. If a primary source is unavailable, the row is downgraded to MEDIUM and a "Verify against [enacted budget bill / agency award ledger]" note is added.
